About Me

I'm a frontend engineer based in Mohali, Punjab, focused on building scalable, accessible UI systems for products used by real people. My work lives at the intersection of clean architecture, engineering discipline, and genuine attention to craft.

Currently at The Iron Network / BookingKoala — a platform serving 2,000–5,000+ active merchants. I've migrated a legacy Website Builder to a standalone modular architecture, built SSR booking flows on a shared monorepo, and shipped custom linting infrastructure that enforces team conventions before code ever reaches review.

I think in systems first. Before writing a single component, I ask: what's the module boundary? What's the shared contract? How does this scale without becoming a maintenance burden? Architecture before aesthetics — always.

Outside of engineering I'm a former university volleyball player — Vice-Captain, competed at NIT Kurukshetra and TIET Patiala. That same instinct drives how I work: systems thinking, team coordination, and showing up prepared. It's also why I built SportSync — because I missed the community after college and there was no platform to connect sports-loving working professionals.
